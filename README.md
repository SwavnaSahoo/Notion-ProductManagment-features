# Notion-ProductManagment-features
  Tired of being the single source of truth? A high-fidelity workspace ownership dashboard — the Notion alternative built for PMs who are done answering "who owns this?" Track workstream owners, decisions, alignment health, and execution clarity in one view.

🗂️ Workspace Ownership Map
The missing layer between your roadmap and your team. A single-page dashboard that gives Product and Engineering leaders full visibility into who owns what, and what's at risk.

Why this exists
Most teams track tasks. Few teams track ownership. When accountability is unclear, decisions stall, workstreams drift, and nobody knows who to escalate to. This dashboard fixes that.

What's inside

•Ownership Map — every workstream, every owner, every status at a glance
•Unassigned View — instantly surface gaps before they become blockers
•Decision Log — a living record of open, pending, and resolved decisions with full context
•Alignment Health Dashboard — risk scoring per workstream with one-click escalation
•Execution Clarity View — roadmap, responsibility matrix, blockers, and dependencies in one place
•Owner Profiles — contact info, owned workstreams, and recent activity without digging through Slack
